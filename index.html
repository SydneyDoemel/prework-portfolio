About

Born in Marietta, GA, Sydney Doemel (b.1996) is an acoustic and electro-acoustic composer and violinist. Her music has been performed by Transient Canvas, The Bent Frequency Duo, Sonic Apricity, The Departure Duo, and The UGA Symphony Orchestra, with premieres in Alba Italy, Prague, Vienna, and Salzburg. In 2021, her piece “In the Teeth Of” was performed on Transient Canvas’ virtual tour at Virginia Tech, Miami University, Hamilton College, and Lawrence University. Sydney completed her Master’s in Music Composition at the University of Miami, where she was the Student Composer-in-Residence for Seraphic Fire’s 2021-2022 season. Sydney also holds degrees in Music Composition and Violin Performance from the University of Georgia, where she was the 2018-19 Student Composer-in-Residence with the UGA Symphony Orchestra; her work “Synapse” was performed in UGA’s Hugh Hodgson Concert Hall.
